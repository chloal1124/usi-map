Global Urban Affordability & Stress Analysis 2026

The Urban Stress Index (USI) is an interactive platform for comparing the structural cost of living across global cities. By visualizing the balance between net salaries and essential expenses like city-center rent and food costs, we help you identify where income provides the highest purchasing power.
