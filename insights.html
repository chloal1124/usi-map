Urban Affordability Analysis: Methodology & Global Comparisons

These essays explore how housing costs, food expenses, and income structure interact to shape affordability across cities. Rather than focusing only on price levels, the Urban Stress Index highlights how much of a typical income is absorbed by essential costs.
